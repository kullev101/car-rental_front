About Us
Why Carsharing
Car sharing is a type of short-term car rental with per-minute pricing. It is usually used for short intracity trips. Using this service is very simple.
Less Parking Hassle
Car sharing alleviates the need for personal parking spots, which can be particularly beneficial in urban areas where parking is limited and expensive. Many car-sharing services have designated parking spots.
Cost Savings
Car sharing allows users to pay only for the time they use a vehicle, which can be more economical than owning a car. This reduces expenses such as insurance, maintenance, fuel, and parking fees.
Variety of Vehicles
Users can select from different types of vehicles depending on their needs, whether for a family trip, outdoor adventures, or daily commuting. This flexibility is less feasible with personal car ownership.
